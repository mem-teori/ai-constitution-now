Human-centred education

School Companion Constitution

What is it?

A constitutional framework for AI systems interacting with children and young people in educational environments.

What is the key idea?

AI should shorten the path to human support. It should never replace human relationships, score children, diagnose them, or turn them into behavioural profiles.
