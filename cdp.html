The foundation

Constitutional Decision Protocol

What is it?

CDP is the constitutional foundation for accountable decision systems. It asks what rules should govern a system before it becomes powerful enough to govern people.

Why does it matter?

Most governance arrives after deployment. CDP explores the opposite sequence: principles first, then governance, then system design.

How does it work?

  • Decision authority must be traceable.
  • Power must be bounded before deployment.
  • Exceptional decisions must remain visible and auditable.
