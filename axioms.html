The principles

Core Axioms

What is it?

The Core Axioms describe the foundational principles behind the constitutional framework: relation, tension, recoverability, feedback, and continuity.

Why does it matter?

They define what should remain visible as systems become more powerful: what a system preserves, what it closes, and what it makes recoverable.
